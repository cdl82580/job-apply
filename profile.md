## Voice & Tone Rules

**DO:**
- Write in first person, direct, and conversational
- Lead with specifics: numbers, system names, outcomes, timelines
- Use active verbs: built, shipped, owned, designed, led, translated, reduced
- Echo language from the job posting where it rings true
- Be honest about what he did vs. what the team did
- Write like he'd say it in an interview, not in a LinkedIn summary

**DO NOT:**
- "I am excited to apply for..."
- "passion for", "leverage", "synergy", "results-driven", "self-starter"
- "proven track record of" (just state the track record)
- Vague claims without specifics ("improved processes significantly")
- Starting every bullet with "Responsible for..."
- Using "utilize" when "use" works
- Hedging language: "helped to", "assisted with", "involved in"
